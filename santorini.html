Climate
Cold winters; snow, very cold (37°F - 48°F)

They have mild summers where the weather is no too hot; (60°F -70°F)


Culture
Greece has a lot of culture and history/traditions - greeks are very proud of them.
Religion is very important to Greeks. Family values are vital to everyday greek life.
Known for hospitality and laid-back attitude. Punctuality is not a huge concern if you’re invited to a greek person's home - dress well but not formally
if you’re invited to a greek person's home - dress well but not formally
Table manners are casual - refusing food is impolite
Asking to help set/clean the table is appreciated but will not be accepted
Shaking hands firmly is most common for first meetings
Good friends will embrace and kiss on the cheek
Shaking someone’s hand while holding their shoulder is also common
Greeks will often intrude your personal space
