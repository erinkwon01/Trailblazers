Culture
French people are very blunt - they argue for fun.
They have the ability to argue for three hours over coffee and pastries at a bakery and still be friends.
If a french person doesn’t agree with you, don’t try convincing them otherwise.
They eat a lot of heavy, rich, butter-y foods. Clothing with stripes is not french.
Similarly, they do not bike around with berets and baguettes in the baskets.
They will get offended if you mention the berets - french people think they’re ugly.
They take a long time to eat, don’t rush them. Quality of life is CRUCIAL.
French people are much more formal. French people separate work life and personal life - asking a french person what their job is comes off as intrusive and rude.
Could appear standoffish because they don’t talk about personal life with strangers. France is very conformist. Everyone is politically engaged Everyone smokes.
